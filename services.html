Business Intelligence

SAP Business Intelligence Consulting

Brillosys also provides SAP BI Consulting Services for our clients. With 7 years of SAP BI consulting experience, our team members have worked on a number of projects across various sectors: Retail, Manufacturing and Energy being the main sectors. We provide SAP BI expertise from Data Warehousing on SAP BW, right through to BI Reporting using SAP BW BEx Queries and SAP Business Objects reports.

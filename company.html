About our Company

Background

BrilloSys was conceptualized & developed in 2013 and officially registered early 2015 as a private company. We are a 100% black-owned with level 1 BBBEE rating and is based in Midrand, South Africa. We have experience in many diverse areas of both software development and hardware integration. Our experienced staff of professionals has worked with a wide array of platforms, languages, and tools in the course of our various projects. With the robust competitive nature of the economy where companies are constantly looking for that competitive edge, BrilloSys was established to provide intelligent and innovative solutions that would help its clients achieve this competitive edge and increase their profitability.

Founders

The company's two founding members have 15 years of experience, collectively; delivering innovative solutions, using a variety of technologies, across the sector spectrum. Since the time it was founded BrilloSys has been involved in projects within business spheres ranging from basic websites to sophisticated business systems. BrilloSys offers a comprehensive suite of IT services, which include: Software Development, SAP and Microsoft BI Consulting, Web Solutions, Mobile Application Development and Internet Services. The company services small to medium enterprises ,and is working towards expanding into the larger enterprises as well as government institutions.
